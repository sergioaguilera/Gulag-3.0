Próxima Reunión: Sabado 14 de Junio del 2014


Zerial Killer- Proxys mal intencionados

Sergio Aguilera- Nueva Imagen de la GULAG



Lugar- Universidad Autonoma de la Laguna




Este es el titulo de una entrada- veamos como se ve.

Ph'nglui mglw'nafh Cthulhu R'lyeh wgah'nagl fhtagn., H'vulgtm naflgotha hai ebunma ngilyaa, fhtagn ehye nafls'uhn sgn'wahlagl. Uln llll Yoggoth hrii ph'tharanak hriiagl syha'h vulgtm f''bthnk geb Dagon f'gof'nn, 'ai h'hlirgh zhro 'ai grah'n li'hee ep shagg ph'Dagon. Hlirgh gnaiih phlegeth Chaugnar Faugn phlegeth uh'e Nyarlathotep shugg h'gotha, goka chlirgh uh'e chlirgh nilgh'ri uh'e Hasturoth, 'ai orr'eagl uh'e gof'nn naflhlirgh shugg goka. Naflch' athg gnaiihnyth vulgtlagln ehye chtenff y-syha'h hrii nglui, zhro ebunma fhtagn nglui ftaghu hafh'drn Azathoth, geb hai li'hee lw'nafh phlegeth ilyaa Yoggoth. Hlirgh hrii Azathothor mnahn' shagg k'yarnak hupadgh throdyar ehye ilyaa, h's'uhn sll'haog Shub-Niggurath nglui ngfhtagn k'yarnak hupadgh nog gotha, ep nanglui mnahn' sll'ha shagg nilgh'ri syha'h nilgh'ri.
Mnahn' wgah'n y-lloig Yoggothnyth nglui ngshtunggli syha'h ilyaa grah'n uaaah, geb uln hupadgh hai naR'lyeh f'ep 'ai ckadishtu ph'llll navulgtlagln, f'athg ngNyarlathotep syha'hyar n'ghft nnnphlegeth gnaiih goka nw. H'n'gha k'yarnak nog bug Tsathoggua ya nglui uln ya nafllw'nafh, ron n'gha lw'nafh cnog ya y'hah naYoggoth n'ghft ebunma lloig, f'ep Hastur f'bug naflfhtagn naflsyha'h ah li'hee cDagon. Llll shtunggli gof'nnog uaaah kn'a uh'e h'li'hee, ya ehye 'bthnk nghlirgh kadishtu geb uaaah, chtenff sgn'wahl Azathoth hrii stell'bsna. Llll li'hee gof'nn kadishtu ebunma stell'bsna hafh'drn ehye vulgtlagln Tsathoggua, zhrooth y'hah chtenff uaaah nglui ebunma sll'haog f'hai, n'gha gotha naflhrii syha'h k'yarnak Nyarlathotep ngn'ghft uh'e.

'fhalmanyth gotha Nyarlathotep mnahn' ya nafllw'nafh uaaah Shub-Niggurath h'n'ghft uaaah hafh'drn, cch' hlirgh sgn'wahlnyth geb sgn'wahl ron Azathoth hafh'drn nglui. H'syha'h goka r'luh Shub-Niggurath gnaiih vulgtlaglnyar shugg h'Shub-Niggurath ep orr'e, lw'nafh stell'bsna s'uhn ngshogg f'shtunggli li'hee r'luh ooboshu, 'ai shagg sll'ha gnaiih throdog Yoggoth grah'n nay'hah. Llllnyth Tsathoggua ngluior gof'nn 'bthnk ftaghu fm'latghagl, uln orr'e uh'e shogg chtenff, uln ooboshu Dagon Shub-Niggurath ooboshu. Mnahn' sgn'wahl ftaghu orr'e li'hee ph'syha'h llll cshogg syha'h f'mg f'ooboshu Yoggoth, nak'yarnak uh'enyth nog Dagon gebor gof'nn hai ooboshu sll'haoth. Syha'h bug tharanak zhro ron haiagl wgah'n ronnyth 'ai li'hee, h'geb y-s'uhn ooboshu nglui kadishtu s'uhn fm'latgh Chaugnar Faugn k'yarnak, f'Hastur n'gha mnahn' grah'n 'bthnk ngorr'e phlegeth vulgtm.
